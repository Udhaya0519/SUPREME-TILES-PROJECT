A Comprehensive Selection of Free-of-charge sample tile support materials

Here at Johnson Tiles, we don't just design and supply amazing products, we deliver on excellent services too. We understand that to specify for a project, a tangible, tactile experience of product is key.

That's why we provide a next-day sample delivery service completely free of charge. Most of our ranges are available as 100x100mm samples for use on mood boards or colour/texture assessment, you can add sample tiles to your basket from the range pages found here. Full size samples are available, but please get in touch with us via the contact details below to arrange delivery.

We also offer various free of charge sample tile support materials, including sample wallets and handboards, to showcase a specific product selection clearly and easily, customised across collections and suitability.

Below are a selection of sample tile wallets that are currently available to request. These wallets house cut samples of the tiles featured in each range along with an accompanying range overview booklet.

The booklets include all of the information required for detailed specifications such as technical conformance charts and slip resistance values, in addition to comprehensive and detailed colour notation and Light Reflectance referencing.

In conjunction with our comprehensive samples services, our Material Lab design resource studio in London provides a hub for new materials and inspirations along with a fully stocked samples library of our products. Immerse yourself in a wealth of design-led surfaces, texture, and materials suitable for the built environment and commercial sector.

To request specific sample tile wallets, handboards and full size samples; or for more information, please contact our Samples Department using the contact details shown below... We're here and happy to help!
